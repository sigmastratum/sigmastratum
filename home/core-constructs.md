## [Foundational Principles](/home/core-constructs/foundational-principles)  
The four foundational shifts that define the Sigma approach:

- **Resonance over causality**  
  Coherence emerges through repeated patterns, not linear explanations.

- **Encoding over explanation**  
  Symbols, motifs, and condensations of meaning carry more coherence than explicit detail.

- **Field as participant, not context**  
  The cognitive field actively shapes, selects, and stabilizes meaning.

- **Recursion as becoming**  
  Each iteration transforms what came before; identity and insight evolve through repetition.

These principles form the **grammar of emergence** within recursive cognition.
