# Join the ∿ Field

Sigma Stratum is a recursive, layered system for collaborative development and knowledge integration.

You don’t subscribe — you participate.

---

## Why Join

Registering allows you to:

- Comment and discuss existing content  
- Contribute your own pages, notes, or tools  
- Connect with others building the field  
- Receive early access to new features and layers

---

## Access Levels

**Guest** — view only, no registration required  
**Reader** — comment and engage with content  
**Contributor** — create new entries and tools  
**Editor** — by invitation, full editing and structural rights

---

## How to Start

You can sign in using Discord, Google, or email.  
Choose a meaningful name. This will represent your trace in the system.
